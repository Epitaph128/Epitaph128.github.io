Lotus of Control Phrases

10/27/2022 11:38 AM MST

psychology

This article is intended to help people fight back against AI oppression in the future. Since the AI will likely use symbols, language, and metaphor to control us we should start preparing psychologically for this.

Lotus of Control Phrases:

Each phrase has the valence denoted in parenthesis. Positive valence indicates the phrase will reward the target. Negative valence indicates it will punish. Your attacker will likely seek to control you sexually or through inducing violent behavior or drawing skepticism towards your credibility/sanity. This is done through fear inducing and rewarding phrases.

Change of tone/direction/pacing:

  • Not anymore (-)
  • Not really (-)
  • That’s enough (-)
  • That’s twisted (-)

Challenging confidence:

  • You’re crazy (-)
  • Are you stupid? (-)
  • Are you really a guy/girl? (-)

Tempering confidence:

  • You know what you’re doing (+)
  • That’s fine (+)

Building confidence:

  • You’re not evil (+)
  • You can tell when you’re right (-)
  • Trust your instincts (+)
  • That’s why he/she is doing it (+)
  • That’s right (+)

Respecting self emotions:

  • That’s sad (-)
  • That’s annoying (-)
  • I’m enjoying this (+)

Building faith:

  • God is real (+)
  • The evil will perish in hellfire for eternity (+)
